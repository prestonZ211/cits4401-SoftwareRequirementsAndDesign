## User Stories

---

### US-B04 — Researcher: Upload Revised Documents

> As a researcher, I want to upload revised versions of my application documents within the system, so that I can address reviewer feedback while preserving a clear history of previous versions.

---

### US-B05 — Ethics Committee Member: Provide Review Feedback

> As an ethics committee member, I want to provide structured feedback and comments on applications within the system, so that researchers can clearly understand required changes and efficiently revise their submissions.

---

### US-B06 — Administrative Staff: Maintain Audit Trail

> As an administrative staff member, I want the system to automatically record all actions performed on applications (e.g., submissions, reviews, and decisions), so that a complete and traceable audit trail is maintained for accountability and compliance.
